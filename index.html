Featured Artists

Zara Chen

LIQUID MEMORIES

A multimedia artist who explores the fluidity of memory through experimental documentary techniques. Zara's work blends reality with digital manipulation, creating dreamlike narratives that question the nature of truth in modern media.

Marcus Rivera

CHROME REFLECTIONS

Former street artist turned filmmaker, Marcus creates visceral urban narratives that capture the raw energy of city life. His films are painted with light, using innovative cinematography to transform concrete landscapes into abstract art.

Indira Patel

BETWEEN BREATHS

A poet of cinema who specializes in intimate character studies. Indira's films explore the spaces between words, the pauses between breaths, finding profound beauty in the smallest human moments and transforming them into cinematic poetry.
